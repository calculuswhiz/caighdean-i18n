:teidealNaCaibidle_ga: Na hAthruithe Tosaigh
:teidealNaCaibidle_en: The Initial Mutations

:10-1-title_ga: Ginearálta
:10-1-title_en: General

:10-1-p_ga: Is é is athrú tosaigh ann athrú a dhéantar ar thús focail mar gheall ar inscne, tuiseal, aimsir nó cúinsí eile gramadaí. Áirítear ar na hathruithe tosaigh an séimhiú, an t&#x2011;urú, _t_ roimh ghuta, _t_ roimh _s_, _h_ roimh ghuta agus _d'_ roimh ghuta nó roimh _fh-_. Tugtar míniú anseo ar úsáid na n&#x2011;athruithe tosaigh, lena n&#x2011;áirítear an séimhiú ar ainmfhocal éiginnte i ndiaidh ainmfhocal baininscneach agus an séimhiú ar ainmfhocal éiginnte i ndiaidh ainmfhocal san iolra a chríochnaíonn ar chonsan caol.
:10-1-p_en: An initial mutation is a change made to the beginning of a word due to gender, case, tense or other grammatical circumstances. The initial mutations include lenition, eclipsis, _t_ before a vowel, _t_ before _s_, _h_ before a vowel and _d'_ before a vowel or before _fh-_. The use of initial mutations is explained here, including the lenition of an indefinite noun after a feminine noun and the lenition of an indefinite noun after a plural noun ending in a slender consonant.

:h2-gp-2-5-title_ga: An Séimhiú
:h2-gp-2-5-title_en: Lenition

:h2-gp-2-5-p_ga: Is iad seo na consain inséimhithe: _b_, _c_, _d_, _f_, _g_, _m_, _p_, _s_ (ach amháin i gcás _sc-_, _sf-_, _sm-_, _sp-_, _st-_ agus _sv-_) agus _t_.
:h2-gp-2-5-p_en: These are the lenitable consonants: _b_, _c_, _d_, _f_, _g_, _m_, _p_, _s_ (except for _sc-_, _sf-_, _sm-_, _sp-_, _st-_ and _sv-_) and _t_.

:10-2-5-a-p2_en: The prepositions _do_ and _de_ are shortened to _d'_ before nouns beginning with a vowel or _f_ + _vowel_, e.g., _d'athair Sheáin_, _d'fhoghlaimeoirí Gaeilge_; but this is not done for _f_ + _consonant_, e.g., _do Fhrancaigh_:

:10-2-5-b-p2_ga: Tabhair faoi deara nach séimhítear an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal i gcás abairtíní dobhriathartha ná ainmneacha briathartha lena gcuirtear staid nó suíomh in iúl, e.g., _ar ball_; _ar bord (loinge)_; _ar comhaois_; _ar comhchéim_; _ar comhscór_; _ar comhstádas_; _ar deireadh_; _ar fad_; _ar fiar_; _ar meisce_; _ar muir agus ar tír_; _ar srian_; _ar tiús_; _ar bogadh_; _ar crochadh_; _ar fáil_; _ar siúl_ (sa bhrí _ag tarlú_); ach _ar fheabhas_; _ar shiúl_ (sa bhrí _imithe_).
