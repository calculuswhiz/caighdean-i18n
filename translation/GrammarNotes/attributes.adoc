:row8-definition_en: the main noun (or its equivalent) in a sentence, e.g., _cheannaigh *Nuala* mála nua, tá *páipéar* ar an mbord_; _canfaidh *sé* amhrán_

:row10-definition_en: clause in which there is a relative particle or in which there is a relative form of a verb, e.g., _sin í an cailín **a dhún an doras ar maidin**_; _cá bhfuil an bhróg **nár ghlan tú inné**?_

:row12-definition_en: a consonant that can undergo lenition &mdash; _b_, _c_, _d_, _f_, _g_, _m_, _p_, _s_ (except for _sc-_, _sf-_, _sm-_, _sp-_, _st-_ and _sv-_) and _t_

:row14-term_ga: consan leathan
:row14-term_en: broad consonant

:row14-definition_ga: consan ar _a_, _o_ nó _u_ an guta is cóngaraí dó
:row14-definition_en: consonant where _a_, _o_ or _u_ are the nearest vowels

:row15-term_ga: cuspóir

:row15-definition_en: a noun (or its equivalent) that represents a person (or thing) on which an action is performed, e.g., _léigh mé **leabhar**_; _tá an *leabhar* léite agam_; _léigh mé *é*_;

:row16-term_ga: díspeagadh

:row16-definition_en: a suffix that makes a noun small, e.g., _-ín_; noun to which this suffix is applied, e.g., _buachaillín_

:row17-definition_en: word or word group that qualifies another word that is not a noun or pronominal, e.g., _rith sé go **tapa**_; _bhí sé mall **go leor**_; _bhí sí thuas **beagnach** nuair a thit sí_

:row18-term_ga: dobhriathar aidiachtach

:row18-definition_en: adverb that has an adjective form (with _go_ or without _go_), e.g., _ní thagann sí **minic** go leor_; _bhí sé **díreach** ag éirí_; _d'iarr sí air labhairt **go cruinn**_

:row21-term_ga: forainmneach réamhthagrach

:row21-definition_en: pronominal that comes before the thing to which it refers, e.g., _tá a fhios agam gurb ise *a* rinne é_; _ba mhaith liom *a* rá go bhfuilim an-bhuíoch díbh_

:row22-definition_en: a function that is the same as an adjective, that is to say, to qualify a noun or pronomnial, e.g., _pingin **ar bith**_; _turas **ar an Afraic**_

:row23-term_ga: frása réamhfhoclach

:row23-definition_en: a small phrase (with two words, at least) that starts with a preposition, e.g., _ar bith_; _ar leith_; _den scoth_; _in aisce_

:row24-term_ga: ginideach rannaíoch

:row29-term_ga: réamhfhocal comhshuite
:row29-term_en: compound preposition

:row29-definition_ga: frása réamhfhoclach ar leith a bhfuil brí mar aonad leis agus arb é _réamhfhocal_ + _ainmfhocal_ is múnla dó de ghnáth, e.g., _ar nós; d'ainneoin_; _thar ceann_. <br /> Is réamhfhocal comhshuite é frása réamhfhoclach den mhúnla sin má tá brí eile leis an ainmfhocal i gcomhthéacs eile, mar shampla, is réamhfhocal comhshuite é _i gcás_ sa bhrí _más rud é_; ní réamhfhocal comhshuite _i gcás_ sa bhrí _i mála, in imeachtaí dlí_
:row29-definition_en: a specific prepositional phrase that has a meaning as a unit and usually formed by _preposition_ + _noun_, e.g., _ar nós; d'ainneoin; thar ceann_. <br /> A prepositional phrase of this form is a compound preposition if the noun has another meaning in another context, for example, _i gcás_ (in case) is a prepositional phrase meaning _if_; not a prepositional phrase _i gcás_ meaning _in a bag/case, in legal proceedings_

:row30-term_ga: réamhfhocal simplí

:row30-definition_en: a word placed before a noun or before a pronominal to show its relationship to another word in the sentence, e.g., _bhuail mé *le* hÁine_; _chuir sé fáilte *roimh* na haíonna_

:row31-definition_en: word or phrase in a main clause that goes directly before the relative particle, e.g., _**na mná** a bhí ag caint_

:row32-term_ga: réimír threise

:row33-term_ga: tabharthach rannaíoch

:row33-definition_en: the form _preposition_ + _dative_, used in place of the partitive gentive, e.g., _roinnt den arán_; _beirt de na páistí_; _cuid d'airgead Thomáis_
