:9-1-title_ga: Ginearálta
:9-1-title_en: General

:9-1-1-p_ga: Is iad na príomhleaganacha den uimhir sa Ghaeilge an Mhaoluimhir, an Bhunuimhir, an Uimhir Phearsanta, an Orduimhir, an Codán agus an Uimhir Dheachúlach.
:9-1-1-p_en: The main versions of the number in Irish are the Bare Number, the Cardinal Number, the Personal Number, the Ordinal Number, the Fraction and the Decimal Number.

:9-1-1-a-title_ga: An Mhaoluimhir
:9-1-1-a-title_en: The Bare Number

:9-1-1-a-p_ga: Tugtar maoluimhir ar uimhir nach dtagann ainmfhocal go díreach ar a lorg. Úsáidtear an mhaoluimhir i gcomhaireamh lom, e.g., _a haon_; _a cúig_; _a deich_ (féach link:#sec_9_2[9.2]).
:9-1-1-a-p_en: A number that is not directly followed by a noun is called a bare number. The bare number is used in counting, e.g., _a haon_; _a cúig_; _a deich_ (see link:#sec_9_2[9.2]).

:9-1-1-b-title_ga: An Bhunuimhir
:9-1-1-b-title_en: The Cardinal Number

:9-1-1-b-p_ga: Tugtar bunuimhir ar uimhir a chuirtear roimh ainmfhocal chun líon rudaí a chur in iúl, e.g., _trí leabhar_; _seacht gcupán_; _naoi mbád_ (féach link:#sec_9_3[9.3]).
:9-1-1-b-p_en: A number that is placed before a noun to express a number of things is called a cardinal number, e.g., _trí leabhar_; _seacht gcupán_; _naoi mbád_ (see link:#sec_9_3[9.3]).

:9-1-1-c-title_ga: An Uimhir Phearsanta
:9-1-1-c-title_en: The Personal Number

:9-1-1-c-p_ga: Tugtar uimhir phearsanta ar uimhir a úsáidtear chun daoine a chomhaireamh, e.g., _beirt bhan_; _ceathrar daltaí_; _ochtar feirmeoirí_ (féach link:#sec_9_4[9.4]).
:9-1-1-c-p_en: A number that is used to count people is called a personal number, e.g., _beirt bhan_; _ceathrar daltaí_; _ochtar feirmeoirí_ (see link:#sec_9_4[9.4]).

:9-1-1-d-title_ga: An Orduimhir
:9-1-1-d-title_en: The Ordinal Number

:9-1-1-d-p_ga: Tugtar orduimhir ar uimhir lena gcuirtear ionad ruda i sraith rudaí in iúl, e.g., _an chéad teach_; _an dara bean_; _an fichiú haois_ (féach link:#sec_9_5[9.5]).
:9-1-1-d-p_en: A number indicating the position of an object in a series of objects is called an ordinal number, e.g., _an chéad teach_; _an dara bean_; _an fichiú haois_ (see link:#sec_9_5[9.5]).

:9-1-1-e-title_ga: An Codán
:9-1-1-e-title_en: The Fraction

:9-1-1-e-p_ga: Cainníocht uimhriúil nach slánuimhir (uimhir iomlán) is ea an codán, e.g., _ceathrú (1/4)_; _ceithre chúigiú (4/5)_; _cúig shéú (5/6)_ (féach link:#sec_9_6[9.6]).
:9-1-1-e-p_en: A fraction is a numerical quantity that is not an integer (whole number), e.g., _ceathrú (1/4)_; _ceithre chúigiú (4/5)_; _cúig shéú (5/6)_ (see link:#sec_9_6[9.6]).

:9-1-1-f-title_ga: An Uimhir Dheachúlach
:9-1-1-f-title_en: The Decimal Number

:9-1-1-f-p_ga: Bealach eile chun cainníocht uimhriúil nach slánuimhir a chur in iúl, agus úsáid á baint as an bpointe deachúlach, is ea an uimhir dheachúlach, e.g., _3.1_; _7.5_; _18.49_; _34.023_ (féach link:#sec_9_7[9.7]).
:9-1-1-f-p_en: The decimal number is another way of expressing a non-integer numerical quantity, using the decimal point, e.g., _3.1_; _7.5_; _18.49_; _34.023_ (see link:#sec_9_7[9.7]).

:9-1-2-p_ga: Tugtar rogha idir dhá chóras chun déileáil le bunuimhreacha agus le huimhreacha pearsanta sa scríbhneoireacht: an Córas Seanbhunaithe agus an Córas Simplithe. Úsáidtear an Córas Seanbhunaithe le huimhreacha ó 1&ndash;19 i gcónaí. Is féidir an Córas Seanbhunaithe nó an Córas Simplithe a úsáid le huimhreacha ó 20 ar aghaidh.
:9-1-2-p_en: A choice is given between two systems for dealing with cardinal numbers and personal numbers in writing: the Established System and the Simplified System. The Established System is always used with numbers from 1&ndash;19. Either the Established System or the Simplified System may be used with numbers from 20 onwards.

:9-2-title_ga: An Mhaoluimhir
:9-2-title_en: The Bare Number

:9-2-p_ga: Tugtar maoluimhir ar uimhir a úsáidtear i gcomhaireamh lom nuair nach gcuirtear líon nó ord rudaí nó daoine in iúl, e.g., _a haon_; _a trí déag_; _a tríocha trí_; _céad nócha a cúig_; _míle, ceithre chéad seachtó a naoi_.
:9-2-p_en: A bare number is a number used in counting when the number or order of things or people is not indicated, e.g., _a haon_; _a trí déag_; _a tríocha trí_; _céad nócha a cúig_; _míle, ceithre chéad seachtó a naoi_.

:9-2-1-a-p_en: The particle _a_ is placed before numbers from 1&ndash;19, e.g., _a haon_; _a dó_; _a trí_; _bus a ceathair_; _a hocht déag_; and for 20&ndash;99 (other than the tens), it is placed before the last number, e.g., _fiche a haon_; _daichead a sé_; _seachtó a hocht_.

:9-2-1-p2_en: Note that the bare number is used for times of day, e.g., _a haon a chlog_; _a seacht a chlog_; _a fiche tar éis a cúig_. This is how time is written in the form of figures: _5.30 p.m._; _4.26 p.m._; _13:00_; _16:47_.

:9-3-1-a-p_en: The number added to a noun to express the number of things is called a _cardinal number_.

:9-3-1-b-p_ga: Tagann an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal ar lorg _aon_, _dhá_, _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_, _sé_, _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_, _deich_.
:9-3-1-b-p_en: The noun comes after _aon_, _dhá_, _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_, _sé_, _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_, _deich_.

:9-3-1-c-p_ga: Tabhair faoi deara go n&#x2011;úsáidtear na bunuimhreacha _dhá_ agus _ceithre_ seachas na foirmeacha maoluimhreach _dó_ agus _ceathair_.
:9-3-1-c-p_en: Note that the cardinal numbers _dhá_ and _ceithre_ are used rather than the bare forms _dó_ and _ceathair_.

:9-3-1-d-p_ga: Ní mór an focal _amháin_ a lua tar éis na bunuimhreach _aon_ agus an ainmfhocail. Ní gá, áfach, _aon_ a lua i gcónaí, e.g., _aon chapall amháin_; _capall amháin_.
:9-3-1-d-p_en: The word _amháin_ must be mentioned after the original _aon_ and the noun. It is not, however, always necessary to mention _aon_, e.g., _aon chapall amháin_; _capall amháin_.

:9-3-1-e-p_ga: Ní chuirtear séimhiú ar _d_, _t_ nó _s_ tar éis _aon_, e.g., _aon doras amháin_; _aon teach amháin_.
:9-3-1-e-p_en: _d_, _t_ or _s_ is not lenited after _aon_, e.g., _aon doras amháin_; _aon teach amháin_.

:9-3-1-f-p_ga: I ndiaidh an ainmfhocail a thagann an focal _déag_, e.g., _ceithre leabhar déag_. Séimhítear an focal _déag_ má chríochnaíonn an focal díreach roimhe ar ghuta (ach féach link:#sec_9_3_2_e[9.3.2(e)]), seachas i ndiaidh _euro_.
:9-3-1-f-p_en: After the noun comes the word _déag_, e.g., _ceithre leabhar déag_. The word _déag_ is lenited if the word directly before it ends in a vowel (but see link:#sec_9_3_2_e[9.3.2(e)]), except after _euro_.

:9-3-1-p_ga: Tabhair faoi deara, más aonad brí ar leith an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal agus an cáilitheoir, e.g., _madra rua_; _cat crainn_; _teach tábhairne_; _láthair champála_ (seachas gnáth-ainmfhocal agus cáilitheoir, e.g., _leabhar dearg_) is i ndiaidh an ainmfhocail agus an cháilitheora a chuirtear _déag_, e.g., _ceithre mhadra rua dhéag_; _cúig theach tábhairne dhéag_; _sé chat crainn déag_; _seacht n&#x2011;iora ghlasa dhéag_. Cuirtear aon cháilitheoir eile i ndiaidh _déag_, e.g., _sé chat crainn déag mhóra_; _seacht n&#x2011;iora ghlasa dhéag fhiáine_.
:9-3-1-p_en: Note that if the noun and the qualifier are a separate unit of meaning, e.g., _madra rua_ (_EN: fox_); _cat crainn_ (_EN: pine marten_); _teach tábhairne_ (_EN: pub_); _láthair champála_ (_EN: campsite_) (rather than an ordinary noun and qualifier, e.g., _leabhar dearg_) _déag_ is placed after the noun and the qualifier, e.g., _ceithre mhadra rua dhéag_; _cúig theach tábhairne dhéag_; _sé chat crainn déag_; _seacht n&#x2011;iora ghlasa dhéag_. Any other qualifier is added after _déag_, e.g., _sé chat crainn déag mhóra_; _seacht n&#x2011;iora ghlasa dhéag fhiáine_.

:9-3-1-g-p_ga: Ní shéimhítear _dhá_ i ndiaidh an ailt, _aon_ ná _céad_, e.g., _an dá leabhar_; _an chéad dá leabhar_; _aon dá leabhar_.
:9-3-1-g-p_en: _two_ is not lenited after the article, _aon_ or _céad_, e.g., _an dá leabhar_; _an chéad dá leabhar_; _aon dá leabhar_.

:9-3-1-h-p_ga: Cuireann na bunuimhreacha _aon_, _dhá_ (ach féach link:./chapter8.html#sec_8_3_3[8.3.3]), _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_ agus _sé_ séimhiú ar an ainmfhocal uatha a thagann ina ndiaidh, e.g., _trí bhád_; ach ní shéimhítear an focal _cent_, e.g., _trí cent_.
:9-3-1-h-p_en: The cardinal numbers _aon_, _dhá_ (but see link:./chapter8.html#sec_8_3_3[8.3.3]), _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_ and _sé_ lenite the following singular noun, e.g., _trí bhád_; but the word _cent_ is not lenited, e.g., _trí cent_.

:9-3-1-i-p_ga: Cuireann na bunuimhreacha _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_ agus _deich_ urú ar an ainmfhocal uatha agus iolra a thagann ina ndiaidh, e.g., _seacht gcnámh_; _deich gcinn_; ach ní uraítear an focal _euro_, e.g., _ocht euro_; _naoi euro_, ná _cent_, e.g., _ocht cent_; _deich cent_.
:9-3-1-i-p_en: The cardinal numbers _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_ and _deich_ eclipse the following singular and plural nouns, e.g., _seacht gcnámh_; _deich gcinn_; but the word _euro_ is not eclipsed, e.g., _ocht euro_; _naoi euro_, and neither is _cent_, e.g., _ocht cent_; _deich cent_.

:9-4-2-c-title_ga: An aidiacht leis an uimhir phearsanta lom  --  _Beirt_
:9-4-2-c-title_en: The adjective with the bare personal number  --  _Beirt_

:9-4-2-c-i-p_ga: Má cháilíonn aidiacht an focal _beirt_ agus é san ainmneach*, is san ainmneach iolra a bhíonn an aidiacht agus í séimhithe, e.g., _an bheirt cháiliúla_; _beirt mhisniúla_; _don bheirt óga_.
:9-4-2-c-i-p_en: If an adjective qualifies the word _beirt_ and it is in the nominative*, the adjective is in the nominative plural and lenited, e.g., _an bheirt cháiliúla_; _beirt mhisniúla_; _don bheirt óga_.

:9-4-2-c-ii-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é ar lorg an ailt agus é sa ghinideach, á cháiliú le haidiacht a thagann ina dhiaidh, is sa ghinideach baininscneach uatha a bhíonn an aidiacht, e.g., _tuairimí na beirte díograisí_; _nótaí na beirte misniúla_; _iarrachtaí na beirte cróga_.
:9-4-2-c-ii-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when following the article and in the genitive, is qualified by an adjective that follows it, the adjective is in the feminine singular genitive, e.g., _tuairimí na beirte díograisí_; _nótaí na beirte misniúla_; _iarrachtaí na beirte cróga_.

:9-4-2-c-iii-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é sa ghinideach ach gan an t&#x2011;alt a bheith roimhe, á cháiliú le haidiacht a thagann ina dhiaidh, agus é ar lorg agus faoi réir ag ainmfhocal eile, bíonn an aidiacht san iolra ar lorg _beirt_ agus í séimhithe (agus ní théann athrú an ghinidigh ar an bhfocal _beirt_ ach cuirtear séimhiú air), e.g., _moltaí bheirt mhisniúla_; _tuairimí bheirt cháiliúla_; _obair bheirt acadúla_.
:9-4-2-c-iii-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when in the genitive but without the article preceding it, is qualified by an adjective that follows it, and it is following and subject to another noun, the adjective is in the plural following _beirt_ and lenited (and the genitive change does not apply to the word _beirt_ but it is lenited), e.g., _moltaí bheirt mhisniúla_; _tuairimí bheirt cháiliúla_; _obair bheirt acadúla_.

:9-4-2-c-iv-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é sa ghinideach ach gan an t&#x2011;alt a bheith roimhe, á cháiliú le haidiacht a thagann ina dhiaidh, agus é faoi réir ag réamhfhocal comhshuite nó ainm briathartha, bíonn an aidiacht san iolra ar lorg _beirt_ agus í séimhithe (agus ní dhéantar aon athrú ar an bhfocal _beirt_ ach an oiread), e.g., _le haghaidh beirt inniúla_; _ag moladh beirt mhaithe_.
:9-4-2-c-iv-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when in the genitive but without the article preceding it, is qualified by an adjective that follows it, and it is subject to a compound preposition or a verbal noun, the adjective is in the plural following _beirt_ and lenited (and no change is made to the word _beirt_ either), e.g., _le haghaidh beirt inniúla_; _ag moladh beirt mhaithe_.

:9-4-2-d-title_ga: An aidiacht leis an uimhir phearsanta lom  --  _Triúr_ go _dháréag_
:9-4-2-d-title_en: The adjective with the bare personal number  --  _Triúr_ to _dháréag_

:9-4-2-d-i-p_ga: Má tá na huimhreacha pearsanta _triúr_ go _dháréag_ (ach féach link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]), agus iad san ainmneach*, á gcáiliú le haidiacht a thagann ina ndiaidh, is san ainmneach uatha a bhíonn an aidiacht gan aon séimhiú uirthi, e.g., _triúr mór_; _ceathrar ard_; _an cúigear saibhir_; _don dáréag clúiteach_.
:9-4-2-d-i-p_en: If the personal numbers _triúr_ to _dháréag_ (but see link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]), and they are in the nominative*, are being qualified by an adjective that follows them, the adjective is in the nominative singular without any lenition, e.g., _triúr mór_; _ceathrar ard_; _an cúigear saibhir_; _don dáréag clúiteach_.

:9-4-2-d-ii-p_ga: Má tá na huimhreacha pearsanta _triúr_ go _dháréag_ (ach féach link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]), agus iad sa ghinideach, á gcáiliú le haidiacht a thagann ina dhiaidh, cuirtear na gnáthrialacha maidir le haidiacht atá ar lorg ainmfhocal sa ghinideach i bhfeidhm ar na haidiachtaí a ghabhann leo, e.g., _éadaí an cheathrair bhig_; _meitheal ochtair dhíograisigh_; _airgead an dáréag chlúitigh_ (féach link:./chapter4.html[Caibidil 4]).
:9-4-2-d-ii-p_en: If the personal numbers _triúr_ to _dháréag_ (but see link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]), and they are in the genitive, are being qualified by an adjective that follows them, the usual rules regarding adjectives in the genitive case that apply to nouns are applied to the adjectives accompanying them, e.g., _éadaí an cheathrair bhig_; _meitheal ochtair dhíograisigh_; _airgead an dáréag chlúitigh_ (see link:./chapter4.html[Chapter 4]).

:9-4-3-a-title_en: The form of the personal number and the form of the noun following it

:9-4-3-a-i-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é san ainmneach*, á cháiliú le hainmfhocal a thagann ina dhiaidh, is sa ghinideach iolra a bhíonn an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal agus é séimhithe (seachas ainmfhocal a thosaíonn ar _d_, _t_ nó _s_), e.g., _beirt chaptaen_; _beirt chumadóirí_; _beirt drumadóirí_; _ag beirt bhan_.
:9-4-3-a-i-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when in the nominative*, is being qualified by a noun that follows it, the noun is in the genitive plural and lenited (except for a noun beginning with _d_, _t_ or _s_), e.g., _beirt chaptaen_; _beirt chumadóirí_; _beirt drumadóirí_; _ag beirt bhan_.

:9-4-3-a-ii-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é sa ghinideach gan an t&#x2011;alt, á cháiliú le hainmfhocal a thagann ina dhiaidh, agus é ar lorg agus faoi réir ag ainmfhocal eile, ní théann athrú an ghinidigh ar an bhfocal _beirt_ ach cuirtear séimhiú air agus bíonn an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal sa ghinideach iolra agus cuirtear séimhiú air (seachas ainmfhocal a thosaíonn ar _d_, _t_ nó _s_), e.g., _moltaí bheirt chumadóirí_; _tuairimí bheirt chaptaen_; _obair bheirt saineolaithe_.
:9-4-3-a-ii-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when in the genitive without the article, is being qualified by a noun that follows it, and it is following and subject to another noun, the genitive change does not apply to the word _beirt_ but it is lenited and the noun is in the genitive plural and lenited (except for a noun beginning with _d_, _t_ or _s_), e.g., _moltaí bheirt chumadóirí_; _tuairimí bheirt chaptaen_; _obair bheirt saineolaithe_.

:9-4-3-a-iii-p_ga: Má tá an focal _beirt_, agus é sa ghinideach ach gan an t&#x2011;alt a bheith roimhe, á cháiliú le hainmfhocal a thagann ina dhiaidh, agus é ar lorg agus faoi réir ag réamhfhocal comhshuite nó ainm briathartha, ní dhéantar aon athrú ar an bhfocal _beirt_ agus bíonn an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal sa ghinideach iolra agus cuirtear séimhiú air (seachas ainmfhocal a thosaíonn ar _d_, _t_ nó _s_), e.g., _le haghaidh beirt mholtóirí_; _ag moladh beirt saighdiúirí_.
:9-4-3-a-iii-p_en: If the word _beirt_, when in the genitive but without the article preceding it, is being qualified by a noun that follows it, and it is following and subject to a compound preposition or a verbal noun, no change is made to the word _beirt_ and the noun is in the genitive plural and lenited (except for a noun beginning with _d_, _t_ or _s_), e.g., _le haghaidh beirt mholtóirí_; _ag moladh beirt saighdiúirí_.

:9-4-3-a-iv-p_ga: Is é ginideach iolra an ainmfhocail a leanann na huimhreacha pearsanta ó _triúr_ go _dháréag_ (ach féach link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]) nuair atá daoine á gcomhaireamh, e.g., _triúr bádóirí_; _ceathrar múinteoirí_.
:9-4-3-a-iv-p_en: The genitive plural of the noun follows the personal numbers from _triúr_ to _dháréag_ (but see link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]) when counting people, e.g., _triúr bádóirí_; _ceathrar múinteoirí_.

:9-4-3-a-v-p_ga: Ní théann athrú an ghinidigh ar uimhreacha pearsanta ó _triúr_ go _dháréag_ (ach féach link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]) má tá ainmfhocal ar a lorg, e.g., _athair ceathrar mac_; _máthair cúigear iníonacha_; _geansaithe dháréag peileadóirí_.
:9-4-3-a-v-p_en: The genitive change does not apply to the personal numbers from _triúr_ to _dháréag_ (but see link:#sec_9_4_1_b[9.4.1(b)]) if there is a noun following, e.g., _athair ceathrar mac_; _máthair cúigear iníonacha_; _geansaithe dháréag peileadóirí_.

:9-6-1-e-i-p_ga: Is féidir leas a bhaint as dhá mhaoluimhir agus an réamhfhocal _ar_ nó an réamhfhocal comhshuite _os cionn_ eatarthu chun aon chodán a chur in iúl, e.g., _1/11 -- a haon ar a haon déag_; _a haon os cionn a haon déag_; _7/8 -- a seacht ar a hocht_; _a seacht os cionn a hocht_; _4/15 -- a ceathair ar a cúig déag_; _a ceathair os cionn a cúig déag_; _33/57 -- tríocha a trí ar chaoga a seacht_; _tríocha a trí os cionn caoga a seacht_; _136/82 -- céad tríocha a sé ar ochtó dó_; _céad tríocha a sé os cionn ochtó dó_. :9-6-1-e-i-p_en: It is possible to use two bare numbers with the preposition _ar_ or the compound preposition _os cionn_ between them to express any fraction, e.g., _1/11 -- a haon ar a haon déag_; _a haon os cionn a haon déag_; _7/8 -- a seacht ar a hocht_; _a seacht os cionn a hocht_; _4/15 -- a ceathair ar a cúig déag_; _a ceathair os cionn a cúig déag_; _33/57 -- tríocha a trí ar chaoga a seacht_; _tríocha a trí os cionn caoga a seacht_; _136/82 -- céad tríocha a sé ar ochtó dó_; _céad tríocha a sé os cionn ochtó dó_. _Fiche_ is not lenited in fractions, e.g., _3/24 -- a trí ar fiche a ceathair_.

:9-6-1-e-ii-p_ga: Más 1 an t&#x2011;uimhreoir, is féidir an focal _cuid_ a úsáid leis na horduimhreacha chun codáin a chur in iúl, e.g., _1/2 -- leath_; _an leathchuid_; _1/3 -- trian_; _an tríú cuid_; _1/11 -- aondéagú_; _an t&#x2011;aondéagú cuid_. :9-6-1-e-ii-p_en: When the numerator is 1, the word _cuid_ can be used with the ordinal numbers to express fractions, e.g., _1/2 -- leath_; _an leathchuid_; _1/3 -- trian_; _an tríú cuid_; _1/11 -- aondéagú_; _an t&#x2011;aondéagú cuid_. The form with _cuid_ is usually used when the denominator is a number over 20, e.g., _1/27 -- an fiche seachtú cuid_; _1/156 -- an céad caoga séú cuid_. For the tens, however, one would rather say _tríochadú_, etc. _ná an tríochadú cuid_.

:9-6-1-e-iii-p_ga: Má tá an t&#x2011;uimhreoir idir 2 agus 10, úsáidtear na bunuimhreacha agus foirm na horduimhreach (féach link:#sec_9_6_1_b[(b)], link:#sec_9_6_1_c[(c)] agus link:#sec_9_6_1_d[(d)] roimhe seo) chun an codán a chur in iúl. :9-6-1-e-iii-p_en: When the numerator is between 2 and 10, the cardinal numbers and the ordinal form (see link:#sec_9_6_1_b[(b)], link:#sec_9_6_1_c[(c)] and link:#sec_9_6_1_d[(d)] above) are used to express the fraction. :9-6-1-e-iv-p_ga: De rogha ar link:#sec_9_6_1_e_iii[(iii)] thuas, má tá an t&#x2011;uimhreoir idir 2 agus 10 is féidir an t&#x2011;ainmfhocal _ceann_/_cinn_ a úsáid i dteannta an réamhfhocail de agus an leagain iolra den ainmneoir, e.g., _7/8 -- seacht gcinn d'ochtuithe_; _4/15 -- ceithre cinn de chúigdéaguithe_;
:9-6-1-e-iv-p_en: As an alternative to link:#sec_9_6_1_e_iii[(iii)] above, when the numerator is between 2 and 10 the noun _ceann_/_cinn_ can be used with the preposition de and the plural form of the denominator, e.g., _7/8 -- seacht gcinn d'ochtuithe_; _4/15 -- ceithre cinn de chúigdéaguithe_;

:9-6-1-e-v-p_ga: Más mó ná 10 an t&#x2011;uimhreoir, is féidir maoluimhreacha a úsáid mar seo a leanas: _an t&#x2011;uimhreoir_ (á lua mar mhaoluimhir) + _de_ + _iolra an ainmneora_, e.g., _33/57 -- tríocha a trí de chaoga seachtuithe_; _136/82 -- céad tríocha a sé d'ochtó dóithe_. :9-6-1-e-v-p_en: When the numerator is more than 10, bare numbers can be used as follows: _the numerator_ (stated as a bare number) + _de_ + _the plural of the denominator_, e.g., _33/57 -- tríocha a trí de chaoga seachtuithe_; _136/82 -- céad tríocha a sé d'ochtó dóithe_.
