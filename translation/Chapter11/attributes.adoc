:teidealNaCaibidle_ga: An Clásal Coibhneasta
:teidealNaCaibidle_en: The Relative Clause

:11-1-title_ga: Ginearálta
:11-1-title_en: General

:11-1-1-p_ga: Úsáidtear cónasc ar a dtugtar _an mhír choibhneasta_ (deimhneach: _a_, _ar_; diúltach: _nach_, _nár_) chun príomhchlásal agus fochlásal coibhneasta a nascadh le chéile.
:11-1-1-p_en: A conjunction called the _relative particle_ (affirmative: _a_, _ar_; negative: _nach_, _nár_) is used to link a main clause and a relative subordinate clause together.

:11-1-2-p_ga: Is roimh an mbriathar san fhochlásal a chuirtear an mhír choibhneasta. Más í an chopail atá san fhochlásal, ní úsáidtear mír choibhneasta chun an príomhchlásal agus an fochlásal a nascadh le chéile ach bíonn foirm ar leith ar an gcopail féin (féach link:#sec_11_8[11.8]).
:11-1-2-p_en: The relative particle is placed before the verb in the subordinate clause. If the copula is in the subordinate clause, the relative particle is not used to link the main clause and the subordinate clause together, but the copula itself has a special form (see link:#sec_11_8[11.8]).

:11-1-3-p_ga: Déantar idirdhealú idir _clásal coibhneasta díreach_ agus _clásal coibhneasta indíreach_ (féach link:#sec_11_2[11.2] agus link:#sec_11_3[11.3]).
:11-1-3-p_en: A distinction is made between a _direct relative clause_ and an _indirect relative clause_ (see link:#sec_11_2[11.2] and link:#sec_11_3[11.3]).

:11-1-4-p_ga: Tugtar _réamhtheachtaí_ ar an bhfocal nó ar an bhfrása sa phríomhchlásal a thagann go díreach roimh an mír choibhneasta.
:11-1-4-p_en: The word or phrase in the main clause that comes directly before the relative particle is called the _antecedent_.

:11-1-4-sample-1_ga: Cá bhfuil *an madra* a fuair sí?
:11-1-4-sample-1_en: Where is *the dog* that she got?

:11-1-4-sample-2_ga: Eagraíodh cóisir *dóibh siúd* a chríochnaigh inniu.
:11-1-4-sample-2_en: A party was organized *for those* who finished today.

:11-1-5-p_ga: Is _iartheachtaí_ a thugtar ar fhorainmneach (lena n&#x2011;áirítear aidiacht shealbhach) lena dtagraítear siar don réamhtheachtaí i gclásail choibhneasta indíreacha.
:11-1-5-p_en: Pronominals (including possessive adjectives) that refer back to the antecedent in indirect relative clauses are called _subsequents_.

:11-1-5-sample-1_ga: Tabhair sin don bhuachaill a bhfuil slaghdán *air*.
:11-1-5-sample-1_en: Give that to the boy who has a cold (*on him*).

:11-1-5-sample-2_ga: Nach ise an bhean a mbíodh teach ósta *aici*?
:11-1-5-sample-2_en: Isn't she the woman who used to have a pub (*at her*)?

:11-2-title_ga: An Clásal Coibhneasta Díreach
:11-2-title_en: The Direct Relative Clause

:11-2-1-p_ga: Clásal coibhneasta díreach a bhíonn i gceist sna cásanna seo a leanas. Ní bhíonn iartheachtaí i gclásal coibhneasta díreach --
:11-2-1-p_en: A direct relative clause is involved in the following contexts. There are no subsequents in a direct relative clause --

:11-2-1-a-p_ga: más é an réamhtheachtaí ainmní an bhriathair sa chlásal coibhneasta:
:11-2-1-a-p_en: if the antecedent is the subject of the verb in the relative clause:

:11-2-1-a-sample-1a_ga: Phóg an bhean sin Gearóid.
:11-2-1-a-sample-1a_en: That woman kissed Gearóid.

:11-2-1-a-sample-1b_ga: Sin í an bhean *a phóg* Gearóid.
:11-2-1-a-sample-1b_en: She is the woman *who kissed* Gearóid.

:11-2-1-a-sample-2a_ga: Bhuail carr úrnua an leoraí.
:11-2-1-a-sample-2a_en: A brand-new car hit the lorry.

:11-2-1-a-sample-2b_ga: Ba charr úrnua an carr *a bhuail* an leoraí.
:11-2-1-a-sample-2b_en: The brand-new car is the car *that hit* the lorry.

:11-2-1-a-sample-3a_ga: Socróidh an fear sin an cluiche.
:11-2-1-a-sample-3a_en: That man will settle the game.

:11-2-1-a-sample-3b_ga: Sin é an fear *a shocróidh* an cluiche.
:11-2-1-a-sample-3b_en: He is the man *who will settle* the game.

:11-2-1-a-sample-4a_ga: Bíonn an bhean sin ar an teilifís.
:11-2-1-a-sample-4a_en: That woman is on television.

:11-2-1-a-sample-4b_ga: Sin í an bhean *a bhíonn* ar an teilifís.
:11-2-1-a-sample-4b_en: She is the woman *who is* on television.

:11-2-1-b-p_ga: más cuspóir díreach don bhriathar sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-2-1-b-p_en: if the antecedent is the direct object of the verb in the relative clause:

:11-2-1-b-sample-1a_ga: Phóg Gearóid an bhean sin.
:11-2-1-b-sample-1a_en: Gearóid kissed that woman.

:11-2-1-b-sample-1b_ga: Sin í an bhean *a phóg* Gearóid.
:11-2-1-b-sample-1b_en: She is the woman *whom* Gearóid kissed.

:11-2-1-b-sample-2a_ga: Bhuail an leoraí carr úrnua.
:11-2-1-b-sample-2a_en: The lorry hit a brand-new car.

:11-2-1-b-sample-2b_ga: Ba charr úrnua an carr *a bhuail* an leoraí.
:11-2-1-b-sample-2b_en: The brand-new car is the car *that the lorry hit*.

:11-2-1-b-sample-3a_ga: Chum an fear an dán sin.
:11-2-1-b-sample-3a_en: The man composed that poem.

:11-2-1-b-sample-3b_ga: Sin é an dán *a chum* an fear.
:11-2-1-b-sample-3b_en: That is the poem *that the man composed*.

:11-2-1-b-sample-4a_ga: Ceannóidh an teaghlach an teach sin.
:11-2-1-b-sample-4a_en: The family will buy that house.

:11-2-1-b-sample-4b_ga: Sin é an teach *a cheannóidh* an teaghlach.
:11-2-1-b-sample-4b_en: That is the house *that the family will buy*.

:11-2-1-b-note-p_ga: Mar is léir ó link:#sec_11_2_1_a[(a)] agus link:#sec_11_2_1_b[(b)] roimhe seo, is abairtí débhríocha iad na habairtí: _Sin í an bhean a phóg Gearóid_; _Ba charr úrnua an carr a bhuail an leoraí_. D'fhéadfadh sé gur ainmní nó cuspóir díreach don bhriathar sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí i ngach cás. Is fearr clásal coibhneasta indíreach a úsáid i gcásanna den sórt sin más gá débhrí a sheachaint (féach link:#sec_11_3_1_a[11.3.1(a)] ina dhiaidh seo). Níl an abairt _sin é an dán a chum an fear_ débhríoch toisc nach bhféadfadh an réamhtheachtaí (_an dán_) a bheith ina ainmní don bhriathar.
:11-2-1-b-note-p_en: As is clear from link:#sec_11_2_1_a[(a)] and link:#sec_11_2_1_b[(b)] above, the sentences: _Sin í an bhean a phóg Gearóid_; _Ba charr úrnua an carr a bhuail an leoraí_ are ambiguous. In each context, the antecedent could be either the subject or the direct object of the verb in the relative clause. It is preferable to use an indirect relative clause in such contexts if ambiguity needs to be avoided (see link:#sec_11_3_1_a[11.3.1(a)] below). The sentence _sin é an dán a chum an fear_ is not ambiguous because the antecedent (_an dán_) could not be the subject of the verb.

:11-2-1-c-p_ga: más cuspóir d'ainm briathartha (cuspóir díreach nó _a_ (aidiacht shealbhach)) sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-2-1-c-p_en: if the antecedent is the object of a verbal noun (direct object or _a_ (possessive adjective)) in the relative clause:

:11-2-1-c-sample-1a_ga: Sin (é) an leabhar. Tá sé á léamh aige.
:11-2-1-c-sample-1a_en: That is the book. He is reading it.

:11-2-1-c-sample-1b_ga: Sin (é) an leabhar *atá* á léamh aige.
:11-2-1-c-sample-1b_en: That is the book *that* he is reading.

:11-2-1-c-sample-2a_ga: Cheannaigh sí an gúna. Bhí mé á iarraidh.
:11-2-1-c-sample-2a_en: She bought the dress. I was wanting it.

:11-2-1-c-sample-2b_ga: Cheannaigh sí an gúna *a bhí* mé a iarraidh.
:11-2-1-c-sample-2b_en: She bought the dress *that* I was wanting.

:11-2-1-c-note-p_ga: Is féidir clásal coibhneasta díreach den sórt a úsáidtear sa dara sampla thuas a úsáid i gcás an chéad sampla freisin, e.g., _Sin (é) an leabhar a bhí sé a léamh_. Mar an gcéanna: _Sin an cineál cairr atá á thiomáint aige_; _Sin an cineál cairr atá sé a thiomáint_.
:11-2-1-c-note-p_en: A direct relative clause of the type used in the second example above can also be used for the first example, e.g., _Sin (é) an leabhar a bhí sé a léamh_. Similarly: _Sin an cineál cairr atá á thiomáint aige_; _Sin an cineál cairr atá sé a thiomáint_.

:11-2-1-d-p_ga: más é an dobhriathar ama _uair_ nó an cónasc _nuair_ an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-2-1-d-p_en: if the temporal adverb _uair_ or the conjunction _nuair_ is the antecedent:

:11-2-1-d-sample-1_ga: Inseoidh mé an scéal duit nuair *a bheidh* deis agam.
:11-2-1-d-sample-1_en: I will tell you the story when I have the opportunity. (_Lit. when the opportunity *will be* at me._)

:11-2-1-d-sample-2_ga: An cuimhin leat an uair *a chuamar* go dtí an Spáinn?
:11-2-1-d-sample-2_en: Do you remember the time *when we went* to Spain?

:11-2-1-e-p_ga: más dobhriathar ama amhail _an tráth_, _an mhaidin_, _an dáta_, _an oíche_, _an t&#x2011;am_, etc. an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-2-1-e-p_en: if a temporal adverb such as _an tráth_, _an mhaidin_, _an dáta_, _an oíche_, _an t&#x2011;am_, etc. is the antecedent:

:11-2-1-e-sample-1_ga: An bhfuil a fhios agat cén tráth *a fhoilseofar* é?
:11-2-1-e-sample-1_en: Do you know the time *when* it *will be published*?

:11-2-1-e-sample-2_ga: An cuimhin leat an lá *a thit* an crann?
:11-2-1-e-sample-2_en: Do you remember the day *when* the tree *fell*?

:11-2-1-e-note-p_ga: Is féidir an clásal coibhneasta indíreach a úsáid in ionad an chlásail choibhneasta dhírigh i gcás na ndobhriathra ama sin, e.g., _An bhfuil a fhios agat cén tráth a bhfoilseofar é?_; _An cuimhin leat an lá ar thit an crann?_ (féach link:#sec_11_3_1_g[11.3.1(g)]).
:11-2-1-e-note-p_en: An indirect relative clause can be used instead of the direct relative clause for those temporal adverbs, e.g., _An bhfuil a fhios agat cén tráth a bhfoilseofar é?_; _An cuimhin leat an lá ar thit an crann?_ (see link:#sec_11_3_1_g[11.3.1(g)]).

:11-2-1-f-p_ga: más é atá sa réamhtheachtaí réamhfhocal simplí, réamhfhocal comhshuite, forainmneach réamhfhoclach nó frása dobhriathartha a thugtar chun tosaigh agus an chopail roimhe chun béim a leagan air (féach link:#sec_11_8_4[11.8.4]):
:11-2-1-f-p_en: if the antecedent is a simple preposition, a compound preposition, a prepositional pronominal or a prepositional phrase brought to the front along with the copula before it for emphasis (see link:#sec_11_8_4[11.8.4]):

:11-2-1-f-sample-1a_ga: Déanfar an obair de réir a chéile.
:11-2-1-f-sample-1a_en: The work will be done gradually.

:11-2-1-f-sample-1b_ga: Is de réir a chéile *a dhéanfar* an obair.
:11-2-1-f-sample-1b_en: It is gradually *that* the work *will be done*.

:11-2-1-f-sample-2a_ga: Shábháil mé an t&#x2011;airgead sa chomhar creidmheasa.
:11-2-1-f-sample-2a_en: I saved the money in the credit union.

:11-2-1-f-sample-2b_ga: Is sa chomhar creidmheasa *a shábháil* mé an t&#x2011;airgead.
:11-2-1-f-sample-2b_en: It is in the credit union *that* I *saved* the money.

:11-2-1-f-sample-3a_ga: Bíonn sé ag labhairt thar ceann an pholaiteora.
:11-2-1-f-sample-3a_en: He is speaking on behalf of the politician.

:11-2-1-f-sample-3b_ga: Is thar ceann an pholaiteora *a bhíonn* sé ag labhairt.
:11-2-1-f-sample-3b_en: It is on behalf of the politician *that* he *is* speaking.

:11-2-1-f-sample-4a_ga: Fuair mé an bronntanas uaidh.
:11-2-1-f-sample-4a_en: I got the gift from him.

:11-2-1-f-sample-4b_ga: Is uaidh *a fuair* mé an bronntanas.
:11-2-1-f-sample-4b_en: It is from him *that* I *got* the gift.

:11-3-title_ga: An Clásal Coibhneasta Indíreach
:11-3-title_en: The Indirect Relative Clause

:11-3-1-p_ga: Clásal coibhneasta indíreach a bhíonn i gceist sna cásanna seo a leanas. Déantar tagairt le hiartheachtaí sa chlásal coibhneasta indíreach don réamhtheachtaí sa phríomhchlásal. Úsáidtear clásal coibhneasta indíreach --
:11-3-1-p_en: An indirect relative clause is involved in the following contexts. Subsequents in the indirect relative clause refer to the antecedent in the main clause. An indirect relative clause is used --

:11-3-1-a-p_ga: chun débhrí a sheachaint nuair is cuspóir díreach don bhriathar sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí (féach link:#sec_11_2_1_b[11.2.1(b)] roimhe seo):
:11-3-1-a-p_en: to avoid ambiguity when the antecedent is the direct object of the verb in the relative clause (see link:#sec_11_2_1_b[11.2.1(b)] above):

:11-3-1-a-sample-1a_ga: Phóg Gearóid an bhean sin.
:11-3-1-a-sample-1a_en: Gearóid kissed that woman.

:11-3-1-a-sample-1b_ga: Sin í an bhean *ar phóg* Gearóid *í*.
:11-3-1-a-sample-1b_en: It is that woman *whom* Gearóid *kissed*.

:11-3-1-a-sample-2a_ga: Buailfidh an sliotar an fear sin.
:11-3-1-a-sample-2a_en: The hurley ball will hit that man.

:11-3-1-a-sample-2b_ga: Sin é an fear *a mbuailfidh* an sliotar *é*.
:11-3-1-a-sample-2b_en: It is that man *whom* the hurley ball *will hit*.

:11-3-1-a-sample-3a_ga: Chaill an cailín a cóta nua.
:11-3-1-a-sample-3a_en: The girl lost her new coat.

:11-3-1-a-sample-3b_ga: Ba chóta nua an cóta *ar chaill* an cailín *é*.
:11-3-1-a-sample-3b_en: It is a new coat *that* the girl *lost*.

:11-3-1-b-p_ga: más cuspóir d'ainm briathartha sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-3-1-b-p_en: if the antecedent is the object of a verbal noun in the relative clause:

:11-3-1-b-sample-1a_ga: Cheannaigh sí an gúna. Bhí mé ag iarraidh é a fháil.
:11-3-1-b-sample-1a_en: She bought the dress. I was trying to get it.

:11-3-1-b-sample-1b_ga: Cheannaigh sí an gúna *a raibh* mé ag iarraidh *é* a fháil.
:11-3-1-b-sample-1b_en: She bought the dress *that* I *was* trying to get.

:11-3-1-b-sample-2a_ga: Dhíol siad an chathaoir. Bhí mé ag moladh í a choimeád.
:11-3-1-b-sample-2a_en: They sold the chair. I was recommending keeping it.

:11-3-1-b-sample-2b_ga: Dhíol siad an chathaoir *a raibh* mé ag moladh *í* a choimeád.
:11-3-1-b-sample-2b_en: They sold the chair *that* I *was* recommending keeping.

:11-3-1-b-note_ga: Is féidir clásal coibhneasta díreach den sórt seo a leanas a úsáid i gcás link:#sec_11_3_1_b[(b)] freisin, e.g., _Cheannaigh sí an gúna a bhí mé ag iarraidh a fháil_; _Dhíol siad an chathaoir a bhí mé ag moladh a choimeád_ (féach link:#sec_11_3_1_c[11.2.1(c)])).
:11-3-1-b-note_en: A direct relative clause of the following type can also be used for link:#sec_11_3_1_b[(b)] above, e.g., _Cheannaigh sí an gúna a bhí mé ag iarraidh a fháil_; _Dhíol siad an chathaoir a bhí mé ag moladh a choimeád_ (see link:#sec_11_3_1_c[11.2.1(c)]).

:11-3-1-c-p1_ga: nuair is cuspóir indíreach don bhriathar sa chlásal coibhneasta an réamhtheachtaí:
:11-3-1-c-p1_en: when the antecedent is the indirect object of the verb in the relative clause:

:11-3-c-p2_ga: Tabhair faoi deara:
:11-3-c-p2_en: Note:

:11-3-1-c-i-p_ga: I gcás gur réamhfhocal simplí (_as_, _de_, _do_, etc.) a cheanglaíonn an briathar leis an gcuspóir indíreach, is féidir an réamhfhocal a thabhairt chun tosaigh roimh an mír choibhneasta. Déantar cumasc ansin idir na réamhfhocail _de_, _do_, _faoi_, _i_, _le_, _ó_ agus _trí_ agus na míreanna coibhneasta _a_ agus _ar_, faoi seach (féach link:#sec_11_6_1_c[11.6.1(c)]).
:11-3-1-c-i-p_en: When a simple preposition (_as_, _de_, _do_, etc.) links the verb to the indirect object, the preposition can be brought to the front before the relative particle. A compound is then formed between the prepositions _de_, _do_, _faoi_, _i_, _le_, _ó_ and _trí_ and the relative particles _a_ and _ar_, respectively (see link:#sec_11_6_1_c[11.6.1(c)]).

:11-3-1-c-i-sample-1b_en: It is that woman *on whom* the interview *was put*.

:11-3-1-c-i-sample-2a_ga: Ceannaím éadaí ón bhfear sin.
:11-3-1-c-i-sample-2a_en: I buy clothes from that man.

:11-3-1-c-i-sample-2b_ga: Sin é an fear *óna gceannaím* éadaí.
:11-3-1-c-i-sample-2b_en: It is that man *from whom* I *buy* clothes.

:11-3-1-c-i-sample-3a_ga: Déantar tagairt don leabhar sa chlár.
:11-3-1-c-i-sample-3a_en: Reference is made to the book in the program.

:11-3-1-c-i-sample-3b_ga: Sin é an leabhar *dá ndéantar* tagairt sa chlár.
:11-3-1-c-i-sample-3b_en: It is that book *to which* reference *is made* in the program.

:11-3-1-c-ii-p_ga: I gcás gur réamhfhocal comhshuite (_ar son_, _i leith_, etc.) a cheanglaíonn an briathar leis an gcuspóir indíreach, is féidir an réamhfhocal comhshuite a thabhairt chun tosaigh. (Murab ionann agus link:#sec_11_3_1_c_i[(i)] thuas, i gcás gur réamhfhocal comhshuite a thugtar chun tosaigh is clásal coibhneasta díreach an clásal briathartha a thagann ina dhiaidh.)
