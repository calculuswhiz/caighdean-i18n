:8-3-1-p_ga: Sa tábla thíos, léirítear na haidiachtaí sealbhacha agus na hathruithe tosaigh a leanann iad. Is iad na leaganacha treise, agus ainmfhocail ag gabháil leo, atá leagtha amach sa tábla. Cuirtear fleiscín idir dhá _s_, e.g., _mo phas-sa_, agus idir dhá _n_, e.g., _ár n&#x2011;arán-na_. Mura mbíonn treise i gceist, fágtar na hiarmhíreanna _-sa_ (_-se_), _-san_ (_-sean_), _-na_ (_-ne_) ar lár.
:8-3-1-p_en: In the table below, the possessive adjectives and their initial mutations are listed. These are the emphatic forms, and they are followed by nouns. A hyphen is placed between two _s_'s, e.g., _mo phas-sa_, and between two _n_'s, e.g., _ár n&#x2011;arán-na_. If there is no lenition, the possessive endings _-sa_ (_-se_), _-san_ (_-sean_), _-na_ (_-ne_) are left in the middle.
